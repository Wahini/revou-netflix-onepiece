Foreshadow of Shanks?
One Piece Film: Red

Plus, the moments when Shanks showed up.

By Bhadrika EvanditoAugust 2, 2022

Surely, those of you who keep in touch with One Piece manga and anime know who Shanks is. He lost one of his left arms to saved Luffy from the sea monster, and got 3 scars on his left eye which are still unexplained. The man who was given the nickname Red-Haired Shanks or Akagami no Shanks is a figure who rules the New World and is recognized as one of the Yonko (4 Emperors of The Sea). Even the World Government gave a bounty of 4,048,900,000 Belly for this ex-crew Pirate King, Gol D. Roger. 

Recently, Oda-sensei announced the 15th One Piece Film entitled Red. The film was announced on November 21, 2021, in celebration of One Piece's 1000th Episode. One Piece Film: Red will be released on August 6, 2022. 

A little synopsis of the AniTV trailer, in the film Red will be set with "Island of Music", Elegia. As well as a new character named Uta, The World's Greatest Diva, who performed for the first time live publicly. The Straw Hats, pirates, and navy officers gather and enjoy Uta's singing, and surprisingly at the end of the trailer, Uta is introduced as Shanks' daughter. 

As if to be a special arc that will flashback to this mysterious Shanks. Based on the manga and anime, we know that Shanks has appeared 9 times:

  • East Blue Saga (Logue Town)
  • Sky Island Saga (Jaya Arc)
  • Water 7 Saga (Post-Enies Lobby Arc)
  • Summit War Saga (Impel Down Arc, Marineford Arc, Post-War Arc)
  • Whole Cake Island Saga (500,000,000 Man Arc)
  • Wano Country Saga (Levely Arc, Wano Country Arc)
 

The 14 One Piece films before Red, are not connected to the main story in the manga and anime, and Shanks never appears in the film. From My Anime List and IMDb data, the best One Piece film after the Straw Hat Pirates passed the New World and faced legendary enemies in the era of Gol D. Roger. 

Will Shanks appear in this film? as an enemy or as a father
